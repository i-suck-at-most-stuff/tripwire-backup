Who Are We?

The team of developers behind Tripwire consists of Christopher Li, Joshua Worthington, Julien Rodriguez, and Lane Smith. These talented individuals have collectively designed and created a revolutionary app with the primary objective of combating the problem of not knowing how safe you really are.

Throughout the project, Christopher Li played a key role in fostering open communication and collaboration between different parts of the team. He helped to bridge the gap between the technical and non-technical members of the group, ensuring that everyone had a clear understanding of the app's functionality and goals. This contributed to a more cohesive and efficient development process, helping the team to stay on track and deliver a successful product.

Joshua Worthington is a software engineer with a dream for developing solutions that make a positive impact on society. With a passion in web development and a strong sense of justice, he took the lead in coding the Tripwire interface and spearheaded its design and functionality. He worked closely with the rest of the Tripwire team, including Julien Rodriguez and Lane Smith, to ensure that the website and app were integrated seamlessly, providing a consistent user experience across all devices.

Julien Rodriguez was a key member of the Tripwire team, responsible for analyzing data and identifying potential offenders in the area. Scanning though an abundance of websites with information, he narrowed down all the offenders in the 32092 and 32259 zip codes. Julien was a critical member of the team and the success of Tripwire.

Lane Smith played an essential role in the Tripwire project as a backup researcher to Julien Rodriguez. Lane worked closely with the rest of the Tripwire team to provide additional support to Julien in analyzing data and identifying potential offenders in the area. In this role, Lane was responsible for conducting in-depth research into multiple different databases.

Together, this talented team dedicated countless hours and resources to create Tripwire, a groundbreaking offender tracking application that combines cutting-edge technology and the motivation for making the world a safer place. By combining innovation, dedication, and expertise, they have created a powerful platform for improving the safety and security of communities everywhere.

-Team Tripwire
