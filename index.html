What Is Tripwire?

Tripwire is a powerful and innovative app that provides users with the ability to detect criminals and sex offenders in their local area. Through a user-friendly interface, tripwire gives you all the information you need to make informed decisions about your safety. You can easily view an offender's photo, address, and crime history.

Where Does Tripwire Get Its Information?

Tripwire gets it information from FDLE's Offender Search and the National Sex Offender Registry (NSOPW). We also get our info from Local Police Records and Crime Transcripts.

Why is Tripwire Currently a Demo?

Tripwire is currently a demo as it only covers some areas of St. Augustine, St. Johns and the 32092 and 32259 zip codes. In the future we will have functionality across the entire state of Florida, and could expand to more parts of the U.S. based on the legal restrictions in said areas. We also would love to add an alert system and a discussion post page. Expanding or making a new map service and algorithm to automatically put in offenders info, instead of putting it in manually like it is now.

What Cases Can/Should You Use Tripwire?

Tripwire is an Web-App that has the ability to track local offenders, which can you see how safe you really are. Some examples include: Moving into a new neighborhood, checking places were you and your children go daily, etc.
